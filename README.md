# 🛍️ Cluster Analysis: Customer Segmentation & Behavioral

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focused on customer segmentation to better understand differences in purchasing behavior, engagement levels, and channel preferences across customers in a retail and e-commerce environment.

Using unsupervised machine learning techniques, customer behavioral clusters were identified to support:
- Targeted marketing campaigns
- Personalized product recommendations
- Customer retention strategies
- Revenue optimization initiatives

The project combined exploratory analysis, data preprocessing, clustering diagnostics, and customer behavior analysis to generate actionable business insights.

## 📊 Responsibilities

- Applied data visualization techniques to explore behavioral patterns and customer trends.
- Conducted data wrangling and cleaning processes to prepare the dataset for clustering analysis.
- Used clustering diagnostic techniques to determine the optimal number of customer segments.
- Implemented **K-Means clustering** to group customers based on purchasing behavior and engagement characteristics.
- Identified anomalous customer behavior patterns and high-risk customer groups.
- Translated analytical findings into actionable business recommendations.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights Generated

### 1. Profitability-Leaking Customer Segment
Identified a customer segment contributing significantly to revenue leakage, along with a loophole-hunting group exploiting promotional or purchasing gaps.

Addressing these segments could potentially recover:
- **Over 40% of lost revenue**

---

### 2. Customer Loyalty Drivers
Determined the primary factors influencing customer loyalty and repeat purchasing behavior.

Recommended targeted actions capable of increasing:
- **Order frequency by up to 1.5x**
- **Average order value by up to 3x**
